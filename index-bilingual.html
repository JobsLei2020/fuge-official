ABOUT US

关于我们 About Us

懂生活，选富葛 Live Well, Choose Fuge

重庆富葛实业有限公司成立于2016年3月，注册资本为人民币壹仟万元。葛根是国家卫生健康委公布的"药食同源"植物，既有很好的营养价值，又有很强的保健功效。公司是专业从事葛根育种栽培、产品研发、加工销售的科技创新型农业企业，致力于为广大消费者提供生态、天然、绿色、健康、营养的葛根系列产品，成为生态绿色农产品名牌企业，产品已获得国家绿色食品A级认证。

Chongqing Fourgen Kudzu Industrial Co., Ltd was established in March 2016 with registered capital of RMB 10 million. Company is a professional agricultural enterprises of science and technology, engaged in Pueraria breeding, cultivation, product research and development, processing and sales of innovative, is committed to provide customers the ecological, natural, green, health, nutrition, Pueraria series products, becoming ecological green agricultural products brand enterprises. Company products have been awarded national ministry of green food certification.
